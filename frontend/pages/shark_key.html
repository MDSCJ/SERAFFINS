Slow filter feeder

Basking Shark

  • Size: One of the largest sharks in the ocean
  • Clue: Huge mouth, tiny teeth, slow cruising behavior
  • Habitat: Cool temperate waters and surface feeding zones

Fast coastal hunter

Blacktip Shark

  • Size: Medium-sized and streamlined
  • Clue: Dark tips on the fins, especially the first dorsal fin
  • Habitat: Warm coastal waters, reefs, and bays

Ocean traveler

Blue Shark

  • Size: Long and slender with a sleek profile
  • Clue: Deep blue back and pale belly
  • Habitat: Open ocean and deep pelagic waters

Brackish-water survivor

Bull Shark

  • Size: Thick-bodied and powerful
  • Clue: Robust snout and aggressive-looking silhouette
  • Habitat: Rivers, estuaries, and warm coastal waters

Apex predator

Great White Shark

  • Size: Large, muscular, and iconic
  • Clue: Conical snout and dramatic counter-shading
  • Habitat: Coastal shelves and offshore hunting grounds

Wide-angled sensor

Hammerhead Shark

  • Size: Varies by species, usually medium to large
  • Clue: Distinct hammer-shaped head for wider sensing
  • Habitat: Coastal waters, reefs, and island drop-offs

High-speed sprinter

Shortfin Mako

  • Size: Lean, athletic, and built for speed
  • Clue: Pointed snout and crescent tail
  • Habitat: Offshore pelagic waters

Tail-powered hunter

Thresher Shark

  • Size: Slender body with a very long tail
  • Clue: Tail used like a whip to stun prey
  • Habitat: Open water and continental shelf edges

Generalist feeder

Tiger Shark

  • Size: Large, heavy, and unmistakable
  • Clue: Striped pattern in juveniles and broad snout
  • Habitat: Tropical and subtropical coastal waters

Largest fish species

Whale Shark

  • Size: Enormous but gentle
  • Clue: White spots and stripes across the body
  • Habitat: Warm tropical oceans and plankton-rich waters

Reef edge patrol

Whitetip Shark

  • Size: Compact, slender, and agile
  • Clue: White tips on the fins and relaxed reef hovering
  • Habitat: Coral reefs, lagoons, and coastal shelves
